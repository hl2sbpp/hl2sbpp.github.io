Addons

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ has it's own addons made by the community or by the developers. Each addon is diffrent from each other.

Addon can be Lua Modding, Playermodels, Viewmodel hands, touchscreen buttons configs, maps and more...

Lua Modding

Lua modding is really popular in Half-Life 2: Sandbox++. You can create your own weapons, entities, props and more.

NPCs aka. Entities

NPCs is a subcategory of Lua Modding, because creating an functional NPC requires some Lua coding.

You can create NPC of any kind unless Lua API doesn't have any limitation about your NPC.

Playermodels

Playermodels is a model that could be used for player and NPCs (if you make playermodel work correct with your npc).

Playermodel is a part of personalization. You can become diffrent from others by changing your playermodel.

Viewmodel Hands

Viewmodel Hands is a model with custom texture for hands in first person.

Viewmodel hands is only for the first person and cannot be seen from third person.

Map

Map is a world that has hundereds of blocks, entities, etc.. inside itself.
